Leslie has a Master of Bioethics and a PhD in social research.

She is a member of the Victorian Board of the Nurses and Midwifery Board of Australia and chairs the Ethics and Research Committee at Marie Stopes International in London. As a long-time researcher and successful advocate, she knows more than most about the decisions women make about their fertility and is a recognised expert in assisted reproduction and genetic screening, gender equality and work/family balance as well as health leadership and regulation. As a public ethicist, her trademark ability to make complex issues engaging for a lay audience has been recognised by her many years as a weekly guest on 774’s drive program with Virginia Trioli, a regular Moral Maze column in the Fairfax press and her appearances on ABC TV’s Q & A and Moral Compass programs.
